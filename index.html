About Me

Hi there, my name is Mert.

I’m a graduate of Yildiz Technical University with a bachelor’s degree in Geomatics Engineering. I also pursued a master’s degree in Geodesy at Bogazici University, where I successfully completed all coursework but did not finalize my thesis. During my studies, I developed a strong foundation in areas such as satellite positioning, geographic information systems (GIS), and advanced geodetic techniques. My academic background has equipped me with analytical skills and technical expertise, enabling me to approach problem-solving with precision and adaptability

I’ve gained experience across various sectors, including gaming, IT, R&D, insurtech, and e-commerce. In these roles, I’ve used my technical expertise and collaboration skills to contribute to significant projects in hyper-casual gaming, machine learning, data analysis, and digital transformation. By working in diverse teams, I’ve enhanced my problem-solving abilities and developed innovative solutions that bridge technical challenges with user-centric designs. These experiences have not only expanded my knowledge across multiple industries but also sharpened my ability to drive impactful projects that align with business goals and technological advancements.
